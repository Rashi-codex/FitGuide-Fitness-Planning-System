Health Safety & Awareness

Warm-up muscles ko activate karta hai, blood flow increase karta hai aur injury risk kam karta hai. Workout se pehle 5–10 minutes light cardio aur stretching zaroor karein.

Muscles gym me nahi, rest ke time grow karte hain. Har week 1–2 rest days lein aur overtraining avoid karein.

7–8 hours ki proper sleep recovery aur hormone balance ke liye zaroori hai. High stress fat gain aur slow progress ka reason ban sakta hai.

Proper form use karein, gradually weight increase karein aur sharp pain ko ignore na karein. Pain ≠ gain.

Severe pain, dizziness, breathing issues ya medical condition hone par certified trainer ya doctor se consult karein.
